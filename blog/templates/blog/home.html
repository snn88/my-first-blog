College Application Organization to the Max!

Quokka provides a simple organizational application to make the next big step of your life easier. Unlike other applications that simply have a search tool or a click-by-click college application organizer, Quokka has everything in one FREE service designed BY high school students FOR high school students.

Why Choose Us?

The minimalistic style of Quokka provides for a comfortable interface to organize applications. In addition, Quokka is 100% FREE with no hidden costs. You can apply to as many colleges as you would like without having to pay a fee because paying for college is already expensive enough! Finally, and most importantly, Quokka lets you view all college applications in one window, so you do not have to click back and forth or open multiple tabs to view the colleges to which you applied.
